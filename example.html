agent-doc-collab — try it

This is a demo document. The commenting layer was injected by comment-server.py when you loaded this page — the file itself was never modified.

How to leave a comment

Highlight any run of text in this paragraph with your cursor (or your finger on a touch screen). A small "Comment" button appears; tap it, type a note, and save. Your comment is anchored to the exact words you selected and shows up in the sidebar on the right.

What the agent does

A coding agent watching this document (via watch.py under its background monitor tool, or by polling /api/state) is notified the moment you comment. It can reply inline — its reply appears right under your comment — or it can edit the underlying document and you reload to see the change.

Why this exists

Pointing at the exact span you mean — "this sentence is wrong," "what's the benefit here?" — is a far tighter way to give an agent feedback than describing it from across a chat window. This is that loop, for documents.
